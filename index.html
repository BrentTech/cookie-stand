Our Story

Our story begins in early 2002 when our owner Pat McSalmon had family dinner that went wrong, or so he thought! When making cookies for desert he accidentaly added in some of his famed Salmon Paste into the recipe. He was horrified when he realized what had happened, but as all good cooks know you have to taste what you make. Pat was astounded to realize that the sweetness of the cookie perfectly complimented the bold flavor of the salmon. Now, 16 years later, the Pat's Salmon Kookies fanchise is a Seattle staple and growing everyday. Pat is consitantly stopping by the stores to meet the customers that he serves and personally whip up a batch of his famed pastry as a demonstration for tourists. Keep an eye out for him yourself the next time you stop by!
